Chocolate Pizza

posted on 20 oct 2019 / desserts

For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a sauepan. Add brown suger and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. cook over medium heat, stirring frequently. untill you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let her cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot-over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated suger untill suger is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait untill the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instruction.u.e. let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup mascarpone
1/2 tsp pink salt
1 lb Black Mission Figs
1/2 cup brown suger
2-4 tbsp water
1 1/2 cup heavy cream
1/3 granulated suger
2 egg yolks
1 lemon, juiced
2 tbsp butter
1 cup honey roasted pecans, roughly, chopped
